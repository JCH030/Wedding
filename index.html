Other FAQ's

What time should I arrive at the venue?

We recommend that all guests arrive at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time. For events beginning at 3:00 PM, please arrive by 2:45 PM to ensure you’re seated comfortably

What is the dress code?

Please dress appropriately to complement the rustic theme of the celebration. For more details, kindly refer to the "Attire Guide" section on this site.

 Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes, parking is available at Kabaleyan Cove Resort

Are the ceremony and reception at the same location?

Yes, both the ceremony and reception will take place at Kabaleyan Cove Events Hall.

What’s the RSVP deadline?

We’d appreciate it if you could RSVP as soon as possible, but no later than January 18, 2025.

Will there be accommodations nearby?

While we haven’t reserved specific accommodations, Kabaleyan Cove Resorts offers a variety of options for guests in need of lodging. Please check their available accommodations for more details.
